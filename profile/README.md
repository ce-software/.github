# Hi, welcome to Craft Education!

We are a data company focusing on expanding teacher training opportunities by enabling seamless data collection and compliance reporting for [registered apprenticeship programs](https://crafteducation.com/what-are-teacher-apprenticeships/).

Most of our repos are private but keep your eyes open for possible future public contributions to things like Learning and Employment Records, compliance standardization and automated apprenticeship registrations.
